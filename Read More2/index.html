What is Astronomy?

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the universe as a whole. It explores phenomena beyond Earth's atmosphere, seeking to understand the origins and workings of the cosmos.

Celestial Objects

Study stars, planets, moons, comets, and galaxies to unravel their mysteries.

Cosmic Phenomena

Explore black holes, supernovae, and the expansion of the universe.

Applications

From space exploration to satellite technology, astronomy impacts daily life.
