What is Numerical Method?

Numerical methods are mathematical techniques used to solve complex problems that cannot be solved analytically. They provide approximate solutions for real-world applications in engineering, physics, and other fields.

Iterative Solutions

Numerical methods often use iterations to approach the desired result with high precision.

Applications

Used in engineering, economics, and simulations for solving equations, modeling, and optimizations.

Examples

Popular methods include Newton-Raphson, Bisection, and Simpson’s Rule.
